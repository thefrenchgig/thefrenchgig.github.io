About the band

The French Gig is a live act blending the energy of contemporary music with the romance of French songwriting. From intimate cafés to festival stages, we bring crowds to their feet.

Available for weddings, corporate events, festivals, restaurants, and private parties. Our setlist moves easily between bilingual originals, classic French chanson, and reimagined favourites.

Based in Düsseldorf • EN / FR / DE
